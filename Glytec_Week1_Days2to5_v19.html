Layer 3SOP-34 — Full Compliance Read
Required before you send anything in Week 2. Know the rules before you break them by accident.
🎓Find in Glytec University — SOP-34 v2.0 (Effective Feb 27, 2026). Complete it today.
⚠️GlytecOne has two products with different compliance rules. Care Enablement (Glucommander) is FDA-cleared — Class II medical device. Performance Insights (Command Center) is non-device CDS software. Never conflate them.
Four Rules You Cannot Forget
  • ✓
    Never describe Care Enablement as "AI" — say "algorithm-based" or "computer-guided"Self
  • ✓
    Never say Performance Insights provides "real-time alerts" or "directs clinical action"Self
  • ✓
    Never promise outcomes — share published study findings, then say "customer results may vary"Self
  • ✓
    When in doubt — use approved language and escalate to your manager before deviatingMGR
