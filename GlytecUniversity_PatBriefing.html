The Why
The Mandate

Day 1 outreach was the original goal. We started with a gap — and built a program.

The Original Mandate
Day 1 Discovery & Outreach
Every new rep sends a real outreach email to a real prospect before 5pm on Day 1. Discovery-first. No demo links. BCC leadership. That's the standard.
The Gap We Found
No Program to Support It
Reps were being handed tools and methodology with no structured path from Day 1 confidence to field-ready performance. We needed a bridge.
Our Positioning
Complement — Not Compete
SalesSparx owns the sales methodology. We own the Glytec translation. Platform language, clinical evidence, discovery practice, persona navigation — applied to our product and positioning specifically.
What We're NOT Doing
Waiting for a Perfect Model
Outcome-based partnerships and revenue model shifts are in motion. We're moving forward. The program is live. The infrastructure is built. Reps are learning now.
